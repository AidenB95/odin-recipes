Prawn Linguine

Description

Prawns marinated in chilli & garlic, served in a tomato sauce & linguine pasta, topped with parsley. Recipes makes two servings.

Ingredients

  • Fresh prawns 400g
  • Linguine (dry) 200g
  • Cherry tomatoes 200g
  • Two fresh chilis
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato puree
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Add your fresh prawns to a bowl & add 10ml of olive oil along with one finley chopped chilli & one finley chopped clove of garlic. Mix & leave to marinate in the firdge for atleast 30 minutes.
  2. Cut the cherry tomatoes into quarters & finly chop one chilli & one clove of garlic.
  3. Add 10ml of olive oil into a pan & heat on meduim heat.
  4. Add the chopped cherry tomotoes,garlic & chillis & cook for around 5 minutes
  5. After 5 minute reduce the heat to low & add 1 tsp of tomato puree. Allow to simmmer whilst we carry out the next steps.
  6. Next, bring one pot of salted water to the boil & add the dry linguine. Cook for 10 minutes for al dente & 12 minutes for well done.
  7. Half way though cooking the pasta, Add 10ml of olive oil to a hot pan & add the prawns prepared in Step 1. Cook for 2 minutes on each side & leave to rest until pasta is cooked.
  8. Drain the pasta once cooked & serve with the prawns topped with the cherry tomato reduction. Garnish with some freshly chopped parsley & enjoy!
