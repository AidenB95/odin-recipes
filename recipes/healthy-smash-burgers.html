Healthy Smash Burgers

Description

A healthier take on a traditional smash burger using low fat beef. Recipe is for two servings

Ingredients

  • 5% fat minced beef 250g
  • American style cheese slices
  • Two brioche style buns
  • One meduim egg
  • Golden bread crumbs 20g
  • One onion
  • One large tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Low fat mayonnaise
  • Fry Light Olive oil
  • Seaonings:Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder, Chilli Powder, Onion Powder
  • Optional: Jalapenos or pickles

Steps

  1. Add your beef mince to a bowl, crack in the egg, add your bread crumbs & season with Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder, Chilli Powder & Onion Powder.
  2. Mix everything together & make 4 even balls. Leave to rest for 30 minutes to bring up to room temperature.
  3. Whilst you are waiting cut the onion & tomoto into thick slices.
  4. After 30 minutes heat a pan on high heat & spray with Fry Light olive oil.
  5. Add the beef mince balls to the pan & immediately press them down with a burger press or saucepan.
  6. Cook for 2 minutes on each side & bring off the heat when done.
  7. Optional: Toast the brioche buns under a grill until golden brown.
  8. To assemble the burger add mayonnaise to the base of the brioche bun and add one slice of tomato & onion topped with some lettuce. Then add one burger patty followed by a slice on american cheese & repeat one more time.
  9. Add the top of the brioche bun & enjoy!
  10. Optional: Add your Jalapenos or pickles before you add the top bun.
